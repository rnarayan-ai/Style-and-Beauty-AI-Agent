# Basic Hairstyles Cheatsheet


- Oval: Most hairstyles suit. Recommend layered cuts, side-part fades.
- Round: Add height at crown, avoid full blunt cuts.
- Square: Soft layers to reduce jawline emphasis.


Products & general guidance
- Use 10-20 ml hair oil for dry hair before styling.
- For light toning use developer 10 vol (5-10 ml) with 2-5 g colorant.
